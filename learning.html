DID YOU KNOW THAT...

Recycling one aluminum can saves enough energy to run a TV for 3 hours. Switching from an old CRT TV to a modern LED TV can reduce energy consumption by up to 80%!

One mature tree can absorb approximately 48 pounds (22 kg) of CO₂ per year. One mature tree can absorb approximately 48 pounds (22 kg) of CO₂ per year!

Washing clothes in cold water can save over 500 pounds of CO₂ per year. Line-drying your clothes instead of using a dryer can save over 700 pounds of CO₂ per year!

Electric vehicles (EVs) produce zero tailpipe emissions, helping reduce air pollution and combat climate change. E-bikes provide pedal assistance, making longer trips easier while still producing minimal emissions. Both options save money on fuel and maintenance compared to traditional cars.

Unplugging chargers and electronics when not in use can save up to 200 pounds of CO₂ per year per household. devices left plugged in on standby mode still consume electricity, which can add up to hundreds of kilowatt-hours annually

Using the right-sized burner for your pot or pan can prevent wasted energy on the stove. A convection oven instead of a conventional oven can reduce cooking time and energy use by up to 25%.
